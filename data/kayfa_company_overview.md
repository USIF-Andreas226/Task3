# Kayfa Company Overview

## Who We Are
Kayfa is a leading online education platform specializing in technology and digital skills training for the Arabic-speaking world. Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Amman, Jordan, we have trained over 15,000 students across 20+ countries.

## Our Mission
To make high-quality tech education accessible and affordable for Arabic speakers everywhere, bridging the skills gap and empowering the next generation of technology leaders.

## Our Vision
To become the premier destination for Arabic tech education in the Middle East and North Africa.

## Core Values
- **Quality First:** Every course is designed and reviewed by industry experts.
- **Accessibility:** Education should be affordable and available to everyone.
- **Practical Focus:** Hands-on projects, real-world scenarios, and career-oriented training.
- **Community:** A supportive learning environment with mentorship and networking.

## Accreditation & Partners

- Training partner with CompTIA
