# Kayf Policies & FAQs

## Refund Policy

### Individual Paid Courses
- Full refund within 7 days of purchase if less than 30% of course content has been accessed.
- No refund after 7 days or if more than 30% of content has been accessed.
- Refund requests must be submitted via email to support@kayfa.com.

### Educational Tracks (Self-Paced)
- Full refund within 14 days of purchase if less than 20% of track content has been accessed.
- Partial refund (50%) within 30 days if less than 40% of track content has been accessed.
- No refund after 30 days.

### Live Diplomas & Bootcamps
- Full refund up to 7 days before the start date.
- 50% refund if cancelled between 7 days before and the first day of the program.
- No refund after the program has started.
- Exceptions may be made for medical emergencies with documentation.

### Processing Time
Refund requests are processed within 5-10 business days.

## Payment Options
- **One-time payment:** Full payment via credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- **Installment plans:** Available for tracks and diplomas — 2-4 installments without interest
- **Bank transfer:** Available for Jordanian and Egyptian students
- **Cash on delivery:** Available in Amman, Jordan
- **Regional payment gateways:** Mada (Saudi Arabia), Fawry (Egypt), Sadad (Jordan)

## Certificates

### Types of Certificates
1. **Course Completion Certificate** — Awarded upon completing all lessons and quizzes in a single course. Free for all paid courses.
2. **Track Completion Certificate** — Awarded upon completing all courses in a self-paced track. Includes project work.
3. **Diploma Certificate** — Awarded upon completing a live diploma program. Includes capstone project and final assessment.

### Verification
All certificates include a unique QR code and verification link. Employers can verify authenticity at https://kayfa.com/verify.

### Accreditation
Our certificates are accredited by the Jordanian Ministry of Education and recognized by industry partners including CompTIA and Microsoft.

## Access & Duration
- **Individual courses & tracks:** Lifetime access after purchase. No expiration.
- **Free courses:** Lifetime access. No expiration.
- **Live diplomas:** Access to recorded sessions for 12 months after program end. Course materials available for life.
- **Updates:** When course content is updated, existing students get free access to the updated version.

## Technical Requirements
- Internet connection (recommended: 5 Mbps or faster)
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari — latest 2 versions)
- For hands-on courses: Laptop/desktop computer (Windows, macOS, or Linux)
- For data science/AI courses: Minimum 8GB RAM recommended
- For mobile development: Required device (iOS/Mac for Swift, Android for Kotlin)

## Support Hours
- **Technical support:** Sunday to Thursday, 9 AM — 6 PM (Jordan time, UTC+3)
- **Sales inquiries:** Sunday to Thursday, 9 AM — 8 PM
- **WhatsApp:** 24/7 response within 2 hours during business hours
- **Email:** support@kayfa.com — response within 24 hours on business days

## FAQ

### General Questions
**Q: Is Kayf a recognized institution?**
A: Yes. Kayf is licensed by the Jordanian Ministry of Education, ISO 9001:2015 certified, and partnered with CompTIA and Microsoft.

**Q: Are the courses in Arabic or English?**
A: Most courses are taught in Arabic (various dialects), with technical terms kept in English. Some advanced courses include English materials with Arabic instruction.

**Q: Can I switch tracks after enrolling?**
A: Yes. Contact our support team within 7 days of enrollment to switch tracks. Any price difference will be adjusted.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Yes. Kayf is available on iOS and Android through the App Store and Google Play.

### Pricing & Payment
**Q: Do you offer student discounts?**
A: Yes. Active university students get a 15% discount on all individual courses and self-paced tracks. Valid student ID required.

**Q: Is there a group discount?**
A: Yes. Groups of 5+ from the same organization receive 20% off. Groups of 10+ receive 30% off. Contact sales@kayfa.com.

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
A: Yes. Installment plans are available for tracks (2-3 installments) and live diplomas (2-4 installments) with zero interest.

**Q: Do you accept payments in local currencies?**
A: Yes. We accept JOD, EGP, SAR, AED, USD, and other regional currencies at current exchange rates.

### Diplomas
**Q: What is the difference between a self-paced track and a live diploma?**
A: A self-paced track is a collection of courses you complete at your own pace with pre-recorded content. A live diploma is an instructor-led program with scheduled sessions, live interaction, project-based learning, and a comprehensive final assessment.

**Q: Are diploma sessions recorded?**
A: Yes. All live sessions are recorded and available for replay for 12 months.

**Q: How many students are in a diploma cohort?**
A: We limit diploma cohorts to 20-25 students to ensure quality interaction.

**Q: Do diplomas include job placement?**
A: While we do not guarantee placement, diploma graduates receive career support including resume review, mock interviews, and access to our hiring network of 50+ partner companies.

### Refunds
**Q: Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?**
A: Yes. See our refund policy above for specific terms based on the product type.

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after approval.

**Q: What if I need to drop out of a diploma for medical reasons?**
A: Medical emergencies with proper documentation are considered on a case-by-case basis.
