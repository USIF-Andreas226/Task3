## Program Overview
The AI Diploma is Kayf's flagship live program, designed to take you from an intermediate programmer to a job-ready AI engineer. Over 12 intensive weeks, you'll work through machine learning, deep learning, NLP, computer vision, and large language models — with live instruction, real-world projects, and personalized mentorship.

## Curriculum
- **Week 1-2:** Python for ML & Math Refresher
- **Week 3-4:** Machine Learning Fundamentals & Algorithms
- **Week 5-6:** Deep Learning & Neural Networks
- **Week 7-8:** Natural Language Processing
- **Week 9-10:** Computer Vision Applications
- **Week 11-12:** LLMs, MLOps & Capstone Project

- Project 1: End-to-end ML pipeline (regression/classification)

- Project 3: NLP chatbot with transformer models
- Capstone: Custom AI solution for a real-world problem
