# Kayf Privacy Policy

## Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, country, and language preferences.
- **Learning Data:** Course progress, quiz results, project submissions, and completion certificates.
- **Communication History:** Records of support tickets, sales inquiries, and chat conversations.
- **Payment Information:** Payment method and transaction records. We do NOT store full credit card details — payments are processed through PCI-compliant gateways.

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve our educational services
- To communicate about courses, updates, and promotions (with opt-out option)
- To issue certificates and verify completion
- To analyze and improve our platform and content
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal data to third parties. We may share data with:
- Payment processors (secure, PCI-compliant)
- Hosting and cloud service providers
- Authorized educational partners (with your consent)
- Legal authorities when required by law

## Data Protection
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Compliance with Jordanian Data Protection Law No. 24 of 2023

## Your Rights
- Access and review your personal data
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications
- Request a copy of your data in portable format
- File a complaint with our Data Protection Officer

## Data Retention
- Account data: Retained for the duration of your account + 2 years
- Payment records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- Chat and support history: Retained for 3 years
- Marketing preferences: Retained until you opt out
