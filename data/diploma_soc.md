## Program Overview
The SOC Diploma is Kayf's most advanced live program, preparing you for a career as a SOC Analyst. Over 14 intensive weeks, you'll master SIEM operations, incident response, threat hunting, digital forensics, and security monitoring — with hands-on labs, real-world scenarios, and a comprehensive capstone.

## Curriculum
- **Week 1-2:** Networking & Linux for SOC
- **Week 3-4:** Cybersecurity Fundamentals & SOC Operations
- **Week 5-6:** SIEM (Splunk & ELK Stack)
- **Week 7-8:** Incident Response & Threat Hunting
- **Week 9-10:** Digital Forensics
- **Week 11-12:** Threat Intelligence & Security Automation
- **Week 13-14:** Capstone Project & Career Prep

- Project 2: Full incident response simulation

- Capstone: Build and operate a mini-SOC environment
